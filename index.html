Discover the beauty of Angkor Wat

Angkor Wat Temple

By CheatChinh | March 2026

Angkor Wat is the largest religious monument in the world and one of the most important cultural landmarks in Cambodia. Located in Siem Reap, it was originally built in the early 12th century as a Hindu temple dedicated to Vishnu.

The temple is famous for its grand architecture, detailed carvings, and beautiful sunrise views. Today, it is a symbol of national pride and appears on the Cambodian flag.

Visitors from all over the world come to explore its history, admire its beauty, and experience the peaceful atmosphere surrounding the temple complex.
